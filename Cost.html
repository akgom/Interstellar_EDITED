LAUNCH:

Fasten your seat belts and make sure your family is prepared to feel a boost of pressure. If you chose to be asleep during the traveling part of this experience, take the SLEEP pill to not feel any uncomfortableness. Remember to consistently hydrate if you feel any type of nausea or vomiting. Feel free to take as many pictures of your travel and post about this amazing experience.

RECOVERY:

Boarding for the return flight will begin on the last day of your trip. All visitors must be onboard by 11 a.m.: stragglers WILL be left behind. Once boarded ensure that your seats are buckled with your helmets fastened. Passengers must remain seated for the duration of the flight for their own safety.

POST-FLIGHT:

After your amazing experience get ready to feel some intense jet lag! Make sure to go home and get some sleep and take time to adjust yourself to your home atmosphere. Try to eat as much protein and vegetables as possible to re establish your basic functions. Your body just went through a surreal experience and it’s time to take care of it! Don’t be shy to take a day trip to the spa in order to adjust to a calm and safe environment.
