SAFETY:

We have airbags issued in all of our launchpads to ensure safety and protection. Any minors or adults over 60 are not advised to ride unless indicated by the doctor. If you are pregnant or have any severe health conditions you are advised to not ride. Pets are not permitted to fly. No smoking is allowed on board.
